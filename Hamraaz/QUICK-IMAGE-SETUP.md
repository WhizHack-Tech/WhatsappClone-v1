# 🖼️ Quick Image Setup Guide

## How to Add Your Army Heroes Image

### Step 1: Save the Image
1. **Right-click** on the army heroes image you uploaded
2. **Save it** as `army-heroes.jpg` in the `pictures/` folder
3. Make sure the filename is exactly: `army-heroes.jpg`

### Step 2: Essential Logo Image
For the complete authentic look, you should also add:
- `hamraaz-logo.png` - **IMPORTANT**: Used in ALL login forms and navigation
  - Should be the Hamraaz logo with crossed swords and "HAMRAAZ" text
  - Will be displayed at full size (120x120px) without any background circle
  - This replaces the emoji placeholders in login forms

### Step 3: Optional Government Logo  
- `gov-india-logo.png` - Government of India emblem for footer

### Step 4: Refresh Your Browser
1. Go to **http://localhost:8000**
2. **Refresh** the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
3. You should see your army heroes image in the main banner!
